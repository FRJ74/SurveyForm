Survey Form

Project Description: Modern Responsive Web Curriculum - Building an Immersive Survey Form

The central objective of this project is to simulate a real-world scenario, replicating the challenges and triumphs one might encounter within a professional Frontend Web Development role. By navigating through the intricacies of responsive design, candidates will grapple with the nuanced art of ensuring seamless functionality across a multitude of devices, irrespective of screen size or orientation. This project serves as a litmus test for adaptability, as candidates learn to imbue their creations with the flexibility demanded by the modern digital landscape.
